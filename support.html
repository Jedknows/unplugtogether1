How can we help?

We want Unplug Together to work great for you and your partner. If you are running into any issues or have questions, we are here to help.

Common Questions

  • How do I pair with my partner? Open the app and create a couple. Share the pairing code with your partner, and they can join using that code.
  • Why is my screen time not updating? Make sure you have approved the Family Controls permission when prompted. You can check this in Settings > Screen Time.
  • Can I change my daily limits?strong> Yes! Go to the Limits tab to adjust your screen time goals. Your partner will need to approve any changes.
  • How do plants grow? When both you and your partner stay within your daily screen time limits, your current plant grows at midnight. Different plants take different amounts of on-track days to complete.
  • What happens if I go over my limit? Going over your limit means your plant will not grow that day. Repeated overages can cause weathering damage to completed plants.

Privacy

Unplug Together only tracks which apps you use and for how long, limited to the apps you choose to monitor. We do not sell your data or show ads. Your data is synced securely between paired devices using Apple CloudKit.
